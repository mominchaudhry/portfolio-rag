# About Momin Chaudhry

I'm Momin Chaudhry, an AI Engineer based in Toronto, Canada. I'm a software
engineer focused on AI engineering — building LLM-powered systems end to end:
retrieval pipelines, agents, and the eval harnesses that keep them honest.

I come at AI from a full-stack and cloud background — React front ends serving
100k+ daily users and serverless Node.js/AWS backends — so I care about the
unglamorous part: shipping AI that's reliable, fast, and cheap to run.

## Value proposition

Full-stack and cloud engineer focused on AI engineering — building LLM-powered
apps with RAG, agents, and evals, on a foundation that's shipped to 100k+ daily
users.

## Education

I studied Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson
University), earning a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computer Science
(Co-operative) from September 2017 to April 2022. My coursework spanned machine
learning, computer vision, and autonomous robotics, alongside the core CS
curriculum — algorithms, data structures, database systems, computer networks,
computer security, software engineering, and computer graphics.
